Learn how to quickly and efficiently implement objects that react to timed gaze input. This video walks you through the steps of modifying the GvrReticle to create customized input systems for your GoogleVR game. Some things used/covered during this video: - GvrReticle / IGvrGazePointer - IEventSystemHandler interface - OnGazeStart(), OnGazeStay() & OnGazeExit() Related Videos: - Drag & Drop Gaze Input for GoogleVR: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gllyv... - Head Gesture Recognition for GoogleVR: https://youtu.be/3icqAJnZF8Y Narration/Voiceover: Roosh Reddy Tutorial & Video Recording: Christiane Snyder
