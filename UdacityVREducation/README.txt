INTERACTION WITH THE ENVIOREMENT
In this project you can interact with some panels/buttons. To do this you
just have to look at them for 2 seconds and the onClick() function will
trigger.

MUSEUM
The museum project consist of and introduction, were VR is explained to the
user. Afeter the introduction you can travel to 5 diferent locations, were
you could see diferent videos, and 2 sample educational interactions (earth
and sine/cosine mathf functions).

At the start of the demo you are at the center of the museum, "the hall".
From the Hall you can travel to other section. When you want to travel to another
section just travel back to the hall.
